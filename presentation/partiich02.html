2. Why SaxonJS on Node?

  • Lots of applications have been, are being, or will be developed on Node.js.

  • The large ecosystem of existing tools on Node.js makes it an appealing platform.

  • JavaScript is not renowned for its excellent XML support.

  • SaxonJS provides full-service XPath and XSLT 3.0 features on Node.js.
