Serves 4.

  • 3 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pkg yeast
  • 1 cup luke warm water
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • tomato sauce
  1. Put dry ingredients into food processor (preferably, though not necessarily, with a dough blade in) and pulse a few times to mix.
  2. While running, add the wet ingredients and let run to knead for about a minute.
  3. Turn into a greased bowl and let rise about an hour.
  4. Punch down dough, divide into fourths, and roll each part out. (It may work best to roll one piece out part way and then let it rest while you roll the rest out part way and then start over. Your choice on how thin to make them, though I usually roll out so that the result is about 12 inches in diameter or so.)
  5. Put on pizza paddle, top with some sauce, and bake in a preheated oven on a preheated pizza stone for about 2 minutes in as hot as your oven will go.
  6. Take out and freeze for later or not.
  7. If frozen, defrost.
  8. Add toppings and cook at 500 °F or so in a preheated oven on a preheated pizza stone for about 3-4 minutes until topping looks right.
