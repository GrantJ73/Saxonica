Serves 2.

  • ½ cup rice
  • ¼ cup broken up spaghetti
  • 1 cup seasoned broth, warmed in the microwave
  • 4 Tbs butter
  • finely chopped “green bits,” chives and parsley, for example
  1. Saute the rice and spagehetti in 3 Tbs butter over medium high heat stirring constantly until the pasta starts to get golden brown.
  2. Add the heated broth and green bits, cover, and turn down to simmer.
  3. Simmer until the broth has been absorbed, usually about 10 minutes.
  4. When done, toss in the remaining butter (don’t stir it in yet) and remove from the heat.

When ready to serve, stir, taste for seasoning, and serve.
