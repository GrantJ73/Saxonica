Serves 4.

  • 1 lb steak, sliced
  • 1 Tbs flour
  • 3 onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup beef bouillon
  • 1 dash sherry
  • 1 lb mushrooms, sliced
  • sour cream
  1. Flour the steak and add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Brown in butter and oil.
  3. Slice the onions and garlic. Add them to the pan. Cook until browned.
  4. Add beef bouillon, mushrooms, and sherry.
  5. Cook, covered for 30 minutes.
  6. Uncover and reduce.
  7. Remove from the heat, let cool slighty, add sour cream.

Serve with pasta or rice-a-roni.
