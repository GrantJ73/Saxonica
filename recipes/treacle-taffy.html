Serves 10.

  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 lb brown sugar
  • 3 oz butter
  • ½ cup molasses
  • ½ cup Karo syrup
  • a pinch cream of tartar
  1. Dissolve ingredients in water.
  2. Boil until the “hard crack” stage (about 310 °F), stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour into an 8x11 inch greased baking pan.
  4. Mark into squares when firm.
  5. Crack when cold.
