Serves 6.

  • 2 slices bacon, cut into large pieces
  • 2 Tbs oil
  • 1 lb beef chunk, cut into large cubes, salted
  • 1 onion, cut into large pieces
  • 5 cloves garlic, halved
  • 1 leek, cut into rings
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup stock
  • 2 medium carrots, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • ½ lb new potatoes, halved
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • 1 tsp worcester sauce
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp miso paste
  • 1 bouquet garni (thyme, bay leaf, parsley)
  • water as needed
  1. In heavy pot render and crisp bacon. Remove and drain.
  2. Add oil to pot and brown salted beef in batches; get nice crust on all sides. Remove.
  3. Sauté onions, garlic, leeks until translucent.
  4. Sprinkle flour over onions and stir to create a roux. Stir in wine and then stock to make gravy.
  5. Add remaining ingredients and meats and simmer until beef is soft, adding water as needed. (~1 hour).
