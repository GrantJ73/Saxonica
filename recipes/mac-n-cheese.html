Serves 4.

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 small onions
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 3 tsp sharp mustard
  • 1½ Tbs flour
  • 1 qt milk
  • 16 oz extra sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • buttered bread crumbs
  1. Saute chopped onions in melted butter.
  2. Add salt, pepper, mustard, and flour to make a roux.
  3. Add ½ cup milk, whisk until smooth.
  4. Add remaining milk.
  5. Heat, but do not boil.
  6. Add shredded cheese to melt.
  7. Turn off the heat.
  8. Place macaroni in a baking dish. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni.
  9. Sprinkle the top with grated cheese and buttered bread crumbs.
  10. Bake for 20 minutes at 425 °F.
